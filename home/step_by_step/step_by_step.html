Step 1

Adjust your desk height so that it is at or just below your elbows – your shoulders should be relaxed.

Step 2

Place your keyboard and mouse directly in front of you within easy reach.

Step 3

Position the monitor in front of you at about an arm’s length away.

Step 4

Adjust the monitor height – the top of the screen(s) should be at or slightly below your eye height.

Step 5

Keep your legs, torso, neck and head approximately in line and vertical – don’t slouch, lean or twist to the side.

Step 6

Don’t stand for too long – change your posture every few minutes and take regular micro-breaks away from your workstation.
